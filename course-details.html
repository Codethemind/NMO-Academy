Master Islamic Jurisprudence with Fathul Mu’een

The Fiqh course, part of NMO Academy’s Rabiya Diploma, offers an in-depth exploration of Islamic jurisprudence through the classical text Fathul Mu’een by Imam Malik bin Anas. This course is designed specifically for women, providing a structured and accessible way to learn the rules and principles governing daily life, worship, and transactions in Islam.

Led by experienced female mentors, the course combines live Google Meet classes, recorded sessions, and WhatsApp support for a flexible learning experience. Students will gain a deep understanding of essential Islamic rulings, including those related to prayer, fasting, zakat, and family matters, empowering them to live in accordance with Islamic teachings.

Course Overview

A foundational course in Islamic jurisprudence for women, based on the classical text Fathul Mu’een.

This course provides a comprehensive study of Fiqh, covering the rules and regulations of Islamic practices. Students will explore the principles of worship, transactions, and family life, guided by experienced female mentors. The course is part of the 6-month Rabiya Diploma, delivered through live and recorded sessions for accessibility.

Learning Objectives

Equip women with practical and theoretical knowledge of Islamic jurisprudence.

- Understand the principles of Islamic rulings in worship and daily life.
- Apply Fiqh concepts to personal and family matters.
- Develop confidence in practicing Islam according to authentic sources.
- Enhance spiritual connection through structured learning.

Course Structure

A blend of live classes, recorded sessions, and interactive support.

- Weekly live classes via Google Meet for interactive learning.
- Recorded sessions for flexible access.
- Weekly assignments and exams to track progress.
- Dedicated WhatsApp group for study notes and mentor guidance.

Key Topics

Core areas of study from Fathul Mu’een.

- Purification and Prayer (Salah).
- Fasting and Zakat.
- Marriage and Family Laws.
- Transactions and Islamic Contracts.
- Ethical Conduct in Daily Life.

Learning Support

Comprehensive support for a seamless learning experience.

- Guidance from experienced female mentors.
- Study notes and resources provided via WhatsApp.
- Access to recorded sessions for missed classes.
- Certificate awarded upon successful completion.
