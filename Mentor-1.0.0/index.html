About NMO Academy

Nafeesathul Misriya Online Academy (NMO) is dedicated to empowering women with basic Islamic knowledge through structured, accessible, and spiritually enriching online programs.

  • 6-month online diploma program specially designed for women of all ages.
  • Live classes via Google Meet and recorded sessions for those who miss any.
  • All classes are led by experienced female mentors and include study notes and guidance through WhatsApp groups.

Why Choose NMO Academy?

NMO Academy offers a spiritually enriching and accessible Islamic education experience tailored exclusively for women. The Rabiya Course empowers learners to study classical Islamic texts in a simple and structured way, guided by qualified female mentors.
