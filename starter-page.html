Getting Started with NMO Academy

Welcome to your first step towards mastering Islamic knowledge with the Rabiya Diploma.

This page serves as a starting point for your journey with NMO Academy. The Rabiya Diploma in Basic Islamic Studies is designed for women, offering courses in Fiqh, Hadith, Tafsir, Seerah, and Home Science. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Explore Courses: Visit our Courses page to learn about the curriculum.
  2. Check Pricing: Review the enrollment fees on our Pricing page.
  3. Register: Enroll via our online form at Google Form.
  4. Contact Us: Have questions? Reach out at Contact or call +91 92071 10017.

Start your learning today and join our community of empowered learners!
